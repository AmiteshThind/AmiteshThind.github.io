About Me

I am currently in my 3rd year of Computer Engineering and Society at McMaster. You may be wondering what is Engineering and Society? Well I’d love to explain as this something a lot of people find intriguing. The society aspect of my program revolves around learning about ways engineers can affect the community around them and be proactive in a sustainable manner.We work closely with the local community to ensure that as engineers we are able to create a positive impact within our society. I chose this path because I am deeply passionate about technology and social justice.A long term goal that I am constantly striving for is to use my knowledge and aspiration for technology to create a positive impact on society. 

This past summer I worked as an IT Analyst at Rogers Communications which exposed me to the technical and business aspects of a workplace. By working on a IT project at Rogers I was constantly working with different individuals from different departments on a day to day basis. This helped hone not only my programming skills but also my communication, teamwork and leadership skills. As an individual I am constantly striving for greatness and I hope that in this path that life takes me, I continue to meet great individuals whose experiences and wisdom I can learn from to make the impact I desire.

Projects
